Who We Are

Welcome to Indian Vegan Kitchen, where we are passionate about bringing the vibrant
flavors of India to your table in a loving, compassionate, and especially the plant-based
way. Founded by Priti, a lifelong foodie and talented chef, our mission is to provide
delicious, aromatic, and homestyle vegan Indian food in New Jersey. At Indian Vegan
Kitchen, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together.
Using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients, we craft meals that are both healthy and
satisfying, ensuring every bite is a celebration of taste and wellness. Our menu features a
variety of classic Indian dishes, all crafted with care and love to preserve their authentic
flavors. Whether you're a longtime vegan or simply exploring new flavors, we invite you to
experience the joy of vegan Indian cuisine with us.
